Ubuntu retail partners

Computers with Ubuntu pre-loaded are available globally

We partner with the world’s biggest manufacturers to optimise and certify Ubuntu on their laptops and PCs. And we work with them and retailers to ensure home users, students, the self-employed, small businesses and everyone else has access to Ubuntu’s security, speed and stability, from wherever they are. As a result Ubuntu pre-installed computers now sell in all the world’s major markets.

  • 500 Bodega Aurrera stores in Mexico carry Ubuntu laptops thanks to our partnership with parent company Wal-Mart.
  • Online stores from Amazon, Dell, HP and ASUS offer a choice of laptops to US consumers.
  • Our partnership with Dell brings Ubuntu computers to 420 stores in Thailand.
  • We currently have e-tail presence with Citilink and Ulmart in Russia. Shortly, we will have presence across 150 retail shops as well.
  • 2,500 stores In China carry Ubuntu laptops as a result of our partnerships with HP and Dell.
  • 800 Dell stores across India retail Ubuntu laptops and now across 100 large format retail Croma and Reliance locations.
